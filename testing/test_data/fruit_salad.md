## Fruit Salad

### Yield: 6 servings

### Ingredients
  - apple: 1 each
  - banana: 1 
  - orange: 1 
    - organic
  - grapes: 1 cup

### Steps
  - Cut the apple into cubes.
  - Cut the banana into slices.
  - Peel the orange, and divide into segments.
  - Combine all ingredients in a bowl.
  - Mix to combine.
